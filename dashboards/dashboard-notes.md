# SmartBudget Analytics Dashboard

## Dashboard Goals
The dashboard helps users:
- monitor spending habits,
- identify major expense categories,
- track recurring payments,
- control monthly budget usage.

---

## Key Metrics
- Total monthly spending
- Average transaction amount
- Top spending categories
- Spending by payment type
- Subscription expenses

---

## Planned Visualizations

### 1. Spending by Category
Bar chart showing total expenses by category.
### 2. Payment Type Distribution
Pie chart for Card vs Cash payments.
### 3. Monthly Spending Trend
Line chart showing spending dynamics over time.
### 4. Largest Transactions
Table of highest-value transactions.
### 5. Subscription Monitoring
Monthly recurring payments overview.

---

## Business Value
The dashboard improves financial visibility and helps users make better budgeting decisions.
